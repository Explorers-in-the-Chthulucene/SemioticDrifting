Goal Identification

The very first creative brief for this website has been thought with a varied and heterogeneous community/user personas in mind. In this sense, the main objective has been to reach as many people as possible interested in Haraway production's main points. Nevertheless, being the website hosted in Github, we knew from the beginning that this could have been a project relying more on the word of mouth than the SEO itself. The realization of the work, from content management to ontology modelling, has been finalizaed for the exam in Knowledge Representation and Extraction at University of Bologna. After gathering the requirements and making the proposal to professor Gangemi [lik somewhere], we decided to extend this digital environment, making it appealing not only from a technical point of view, with detailed insights about the workflow process for the ontology modelling and knowledge graph creation [link], but also to provide a catchy user flow for the one interested in Donna Haraway's legacy.
